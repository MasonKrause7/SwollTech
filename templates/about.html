Data Driven Progress

SwollTech aims to give users free data analysis tools to drive progress in their fitness regimen.

Every modern day business and system uses data to optimize operations, however when it comes to individual fitness, most people are not tracking their workouts, or keep track of workouts in notebooks which are hard to derive useful data points from. SwollTech is the solution, allowing you to build your own custom workouts and start keeping track of your data, set by set. Visualizing strategic data points on different types of graphs will uncover your long term fitness trends and help you get the most out of every workout.

SwollTech is still in development, so any feedback you have is greatly appreciated. We want to provide the most valuable tool possible in order to allow everyone to optimize their health and fitness, so please reach out to masongkrause@yahoo.com with feedback.

Founding of SwollTech

SwollTech was founded by Mason Krause in 2023. As an avid gym goer who was working to come back from a shoulder injury, he noticed the lack of useful software in the personal fitness realm. As a student in software engineering, Mason set out to build a platform that experienced lifters could use to build their own custom workouts and start keeping statistics for each set.
